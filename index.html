Privacy Policy – MBBS Freaks

Last updated: 17/01/2026

MBBS Freaks (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the MBBS Freaks mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and login credentials used to create and manage your account.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you interact with the app, such as accessed content and app activity.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and crash reports for performance and stability improvements.

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide access to educational content associated with your account
  • Maintain app security and functionality
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send important app-related updates

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Used for authentication, backend services, and crash reporting
  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In: Used for user authentication where applicable

Data Security

We store user data securely and take reasonable measures to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Account Deletion & Data Removal

Users can permanently delete their account and associated personal data directly within the app by navigating to Profile → Delete Account. Once deleted, the account and related data cannot be recovered.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
